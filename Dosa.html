Dosa


A Dosa is a thin pancake or crepe originating from South India, made from a
fermented batter predominantly consisting of lentils and rice.




Why do we Like Dosa?

Dosas are not just a treat for your taste buds, but also light on your digestive system. Prepared from rice and lentils, dosa is fermented breakfast option, which makes it easy for your body to assimilate and digest it.
